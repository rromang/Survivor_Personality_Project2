Personalities, Ages, Outcomes & Home States

We examined data collected from the popular reality competition television show, Survivor. The dataset we chose is related to ‘Castaways’ (contestants, players, or survivors), and contains interesting information about each castaway such as age, Meyers Briggs personality type, home city/state, and their final result on the show. Final result includes when a Castaway was voted off (e.g. 1st voted off), Sole Survivor (the winner), Runner Up, and less frequent results such as Quit, Eliminated, and Medically Evacuated.

Visualizations

What are the most and least dominant personality types of Castaways? Of Sole Survivors and the 1st Voted Off? What are the age groups and home states of Castaways? These are the questions we attempted to answer through this project.
