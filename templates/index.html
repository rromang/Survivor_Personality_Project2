Personalities, Ages, Outcomes & Home States

We examined data collected from the popular reality competition television show, Survivor. The dataset we chose is related to ‘Castaways’ (contestants, players, or survivors), and contains interesting information about each castaway such as age, Meyers Briggs personality type, home city/state, and their final result on the show. Final result includes when a Castaway was voted off (e.g. 1st voted off), Sole Survivor (the winner), Runner Up, and less frequent results such as Quit, Eliminated, and Medically Evacuated.

Visualizations

What are the most and least dominant personality types of Castaways? Of Sole Survivors and the 1st Voted Off? What are the age groups and home states of Castaways? These are the questions we attempted to answer through this project.

Personality Type Background Info









There are 16 possible personality type outcomes from the Meyers Briggs test which are grouped into 4 overall categories: Analysts, Diplomats, Sentinels, and Explorers. All 16 types were represented in the contestants on Survivor. To explore more about all these personality types click here.

Personality Analysis

The most common personality type amongst all contestants was the Campaigner. Acoording to 16Personalities.com, "a Campaigner (ENFP) is someone with the Extraverted, Intuitive, Feeling, and Prospecting personality traits. These people tend to embrace big ideas and actions that reflect their sense of hope and goodwill toward others. Their vibrant energy can flow in many directions."

If you want to learn more about the Campainger personality type click here.

The Campaigner type was also the most common in those who were voted first to leave. However, amongst the winners, the Entrepreneur personality type was the most frequent. Again from 16Personalities.com, "an Entrepreneur (ESTP) is someone with the Extraverted, Observant, Thinking, and Prospecting personality traits. They tend to be energetic and action-oriented, deftly navigating whatever is in front of them. They love uncovering life’s opportunities, whether socializing with others or in more personal pursuits."

If you want to learn more about the Entrepreneur personality type click here.

Age Analysis

The majority of contesants were between the ages of 26 and 35. It is not surprising that this was also the most frequent age group of the Sole Survivors. There has only been 1 contestant older than 46 to win and they were actually in the 56-65 age group. No one between the ages of 46-55 or over 65 have won the show throughout the impressive 40 seasons that it has been running.
